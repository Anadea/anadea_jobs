We are looking for a Project Manager who can improve processes, effectively communicate with clients, implement standardized approaches to project management, and support the team in strategic development.

**Responsibilities**

* Manage **end-to-end delivery** of software development projects (web, mobile, backend)
* Define and maintain **project scope, schedule, budget, and risks**
* Create and maintain project documentation:

  * Project Plan
  * Status Reports
  * Risk & Issue Logs
  * Change Requests
* Act as the **main point of contact for the client**
* Facilitate and run:

  * Project kick-offs
  * Sprint planning & reviews
  * Status meetings
  * Retrospectives
* Track project progress using **KPIs and delivery metrics**
* Proactively identify and mitigate **risks, blockers, and dependencies**
* Coordinate work of cross-functional teams (developers, QA, designers)
* Ensure compliance with **PMO processes, templates, and governance**
* Manage **scope changes and client expectations**
* Provide **regular and transparent reporting** to stakeholders
* Support presales activities when needed (estimation, planning, proposal input)

**RequirementsMust-have**

* **2–4 years of experience** as a Project Manager in IT / software development
* Proven experience managing **fixed-price and/or time & material projects**
* Strong understanding of **SDLC** and Agile methodologies (Scrum, Kanban)
* Experience working with distributed/remote teams
* Confident communication with **clients and senior stakeholders**
* Ability to manage **multiple projects** simultaneously
* Experience with project management tools (e.g. Jira, ClickUp, Confluence, Asana)
* Good understanding of:

  * Project scheduling
  * Budget tracking
  * Risk management
* Upper-intermediate or higher **English level** (written and spoken)

**Nice-to-have**

* PM certification (PMP, Agile, Scrum Master)
* Basic understanding of software architecture and technical constraints
* Experience working with **PMO frameworks and governance**
* Experience in presales or client onboarding\
