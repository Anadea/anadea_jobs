## About the Role

We are looking for a Project Manager to lead end-to-end delivery of software development projects and ensure predictable, high-quality outcomes for our clients. You will serve as a key communication hub between stakeholders and delivery teams, driving execution, transparency, and process compliance.

## Responsibilities

* Own full-cycle project delivery (web, mobile, backend)
* Define and manage scope, timeline, budget, and risks
* Serve as the primary point of contact for clients
* Plan, organize, and facilitate key Agile ceremonies:
* * Kick-offs
  * Sprint planning & reviews
  * Status meetings
  * Retrospectives
* Maintain core project documentation:
* * Project Plan
  * Status Reports
  * Risk & Issue Logs
  * Change Requests
* Track delivery using KPIs and performance metrics
* Proactively identify and resolve risks, blockers, and dependencies
* Coordinate cross-functional teams (developers, QA, designers)
* Ensure adherence to PMO processes, standards, and governance
* Manage scope changes and client expectations
* Provide transparent and regular reporting to stakeholders
* Support presales activities (estimation, planning, proposal input)
* Perform basic people management:
* * Resource allocation
  * 1:1 meetings

## Requirements

### Must-have

* 2–4 years of experience as a Project Manager in IT/software development
* Experience with Fixed Price and/or Time & Material models
* Strong knowledge of SDLC and Agile methodologies
* Proven ability to work with distributed/remote teams
* Strong client-facing communication skills
* Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
* Hands-on experience with PM tools:
* * Jira / ClickUp / Confluence / Asana
* Solid understanding of:
* * Project planning & scheduling
  * Budget tracking
  * Risk management
* English level: Upper-Intermediate+ (B2+)

## Nice-to-have

* PM certifications (PMP, Scrum Master, Agile)
* Basic understanding of software architecture
* Experience working within a PMO framework
* Experience in presales / client onboarding

## What Success Looks Like (optional but recommended)

* Projects delivered on time, within budget, and scope
* High client satisfaction (NPS / feedback)
* Minimal escalations and controlled risks
* Transparent and predictable delivery process
* Strong alignment with PMO standards
