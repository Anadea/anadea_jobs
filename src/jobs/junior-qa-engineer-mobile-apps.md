---
title: Junior QA Engineer (Mobile Apps)
location: Ukraine/Europe
---
We are looking for a motivated and detail-oriented Junior QA Engineer to support quality\
assurance across our mobile applications portfolio.\
This role will focus primarily on non-subscription mobile apps, ensuring product stability,\
correct functionality, and smooth user experience across iOS and Android platforms.\
The QA Engineer will collaborate closely with Product, Development, and Design teams to\
help deliver reliable and high-quality releases.\
\
\
Responsibilities\
● Execute functional, regression, and smoke testing on iOS and Android applications.\
● Validate core app flows, including onboarding, feature functionality, ads integrations, and\
in-app purchases.\
● Identify, document, and report bugs with clear reproduction steps and impact description.\
● Support release testing before production deployments and app store submissions.\
● Perform cross-device and cross-OS validation.\
● Validate basic API responses and backend integrations when required.\
● Assist in improving QA documentation and test cases.\
● Proactively flag UX issues and inconsistencies during testing.\
\
\
Requirements\
● 1–2 years of experience in QA (mobile experience strongly preferred).\
● Basic understanding of iOS and Android app environments.

● Experience with bug tracking tools (e.g., JIRA or similar).\
● Strong attention to detail and structured thinking.\
● Ability to follow testing processes and meet deadlines.\
● Comfortable working in cross-functional teams.\
● Professional English for documentation and internal communication.\
\
\
Nice to Have\
● Experience testing apps with ads or freemium monetization models.\
● Basic knowledge of Firebase, analytics tools, or crash reporting platforms.\
● Exposure to API testing tools (e.g., Postman).\
● Interest in automation testing (not required)
