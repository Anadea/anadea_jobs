We are looking for a talented Product Designer to join our team and take ownership of the user experience and visual evolution of our AI-powered mobile applications.\
You will work closely with Product, Engineering (Flutter/Python), Growth, and Monetization teams to design intuitive, high-converting, and scalable experiences for a subscription-based AI app.\
\
What You’ll Do\
● Design intuitive and modern mobile-first experiences (iOS & Android).\
● Own end-to-end product design: research → wireframes → prototypes → final UI.\
● Improve onboarding, paywalls, and subscription flows to maximize conversion and retention.\
● Redesign complex AI flows to make them simple and “resolutive” for users.\
● Optimize user journeys for:

* AI chat interactions
* Image generation flow
* Feature discovery
* Error handling & fallback states

  ● Collaborate with developers to ensure pixel-perfect implementation in Flutter.\
  ● Create scalable design systems and reusable UI components.\
  ● Run UX audits and competitor benchmarking (ChatGPT, AI apps, etc.).\
  ● Support A/B testing initiatives with Growth team.\
  ● Ensure product experience aligns with our “all-in-one AI” positioning.

   

Requirements\
● 3+ years of experience in mobile product design.\
● Strong portfolio showcasing mobile apps (subscription-based preferred).\
● Experience designing AI, SaaS, or productivity apps is a strong plus.\
● Proficiency in Figma (components, auto-layout, design systems).\
● Strong understanding of:

* UX principles
* Conversion optimization
* Behavioral design
* Subscription/paywall psychology\
  ● Experience designing for both iOS and Android guidelines.\
  ● Ability to simplify complex flows into clear user journeys.\
  ● Strong attention to detail.\
  ● Ability to work independently and take ownership of product areas.\
  ● Good English communication skills.\
  \
  Bonus Points\
  ● Experience with AI tools or LLM-based applications.\
  ● Experience designing for in-app purchases and monetization flows.\
  ● Experience with growth experiments and A/B testing.\
  ● Understanding of analytics tools (Amplitude, Firebase, etc.).\
  ● Motion design or micro-interaction experience.\
  ● Experience in fast-moving startup or M&A environments.
